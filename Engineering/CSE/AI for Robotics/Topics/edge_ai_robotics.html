    Edge AI in Robotics

    What is Edge AI Robotics?

    Edge AI in robotics refers to deploying artificial intelligence algorithms directly on robotic systems at the "edge" of the network, enabling real-time processing without relying on cloud connectivity. This approach reduces latency, enhances privacy, and allows operation in disconnected environments.
