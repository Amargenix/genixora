    1. Face Detection

    Methods

    • Haar Cascades: Traditional approach
    • HOG + SVM: Histogram of Oriented Gradients
    • DNN-based: MTCNN, YuNet
    • YOLO variants: Real-time detection

    Performance Comparison

    Method Accuracy FPS
    Haar Cascade 78% 25
    MTCNN 95% 12
    YuNet 93% 60
    Recommendation: YuNet offers best balance for robotics applications

    3. Face Matching

    Techniques

    • Cosine Similarity: Most common
    • Euclidean Distance: Simple metric
    • k-NN Classifier: For multiple faces
    • Siamese Networks: Direct comparison

    Thresholds

    • Same person: >0.6 cosine
    • Different people: <0.4 cosine
    • Uncertain: 0.4-0.6 range
    Note: Thresholds vary by model and application
