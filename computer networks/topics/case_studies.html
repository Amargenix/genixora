    This document presents detailed case studies of computer network implementations across various industries and scenarios. Each case study examines the network requirements, design decisions, implementation challenges, and outcomes.

    Case Study 1: Google's Global Network Infrastructure

    Background

    Google operates one of the largest and most sophisticated computer networks in the world, connecting data centers across continents with high-speed fiber optic cables.

    Network Architecture

    • Software-defined networking (SDN) approach
    • Private fiber network spanning continents
    • Edge caching servers worldwide
    • Load balancing across multiple data centers

    Key Challenges

    • Maintaining low latency for global services
    • Handling massive traffic spikes
    • Network security at scale
    • Cost-effective bandwidth utilization

    Solutions Implemented

    Google developed the B4 SDN network for inter-data center connectivity, achieving higher utilization rates than traditional networks. They implemented:

    • Centralized traffic engineering
    • Dynamic bandwidth allocation
    • Advanced congestion control algorithms

    Results

    • Average link utilization increased from 30-40% to 70-80%
    • Significant cost savings in bandwidth
    • Improved reliability through automated failover

    Case Study 2: NYSE Network Infrastructure

    Background

    The New York Stock Exchange requires ultra-low latency, high reliability networking to support electronic trading.

    Network Requirements

    • Sub-millisecond latency for trading systems
    • 99.999% availability
    • Secure transmission of financial data
    • Support for massive message volumes

    Architecture

    • Dedicated fiber optic network
    • Co-location facilities for trading firms
    • Redundant network paths with automatic failover
    • Multicast distribution for market data

    Results

    • Average latency of 50 microseconds
    • Zero unscheduled outages in past 5 years
    • Handles peak loads of 5 million messages/second
